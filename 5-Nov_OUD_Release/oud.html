IMPORTANT TREATMENT CONSIDERATIONS - PREGNANCY

Pregnant women can only be treated with buprenorphine monotherapy (available in Healthy Beginnings or methadone).
Healthy Beginnings

All pregnant women should be referred to Healthy Beginnings. Pregnant women can only be treated with buprenorphine monotherapy (available in Healthy Beginnings or methadone)

Oral Naltrexone

Note: oral naltrexone isn’t as effective as IM naltrexone (availability in specialty care), suboxone or methadone.

Suboxone in primary care

Suboxone is contraindicated in pregnancy. Please refer to healthy beginnings.
